What is React?

React represents the concept of responding to stimuli, whether in technology, science, or daily life.

Learn More

Introduction

The word "react" originates from Latin, meaning "to perform again." It commonly refers to a response to stimuli, whether physical, emotional, or intellectual.

Contexts of 'React'

Software Development

React is a popular JavaScript library for building dynamic user interfaces with reusable components.

Human Behavior

In psychology, reacting refers to how people respond to external stimuli, such as emotions or social interactions.

Derivative Forms of 'React'

Reaction

A response to a specific stimulus or event, whether physical or emotional.

Reactive

Describes something prone to immediate response, often without prior thought.

Reactivity

The property of being able to react to a stimulus or change.

Reactively

An adverb describing how an action is performed in response to something.
